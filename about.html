About The Brand

Pretty & Paid was created for women carrying a room they have not been shown how to build.

The brand exists for women who know the assignment is real, but need the strategy, structure, and confidence to bring that room into the world with excellence.

The Story

The method came from real event rooms, not borrowed language.

Real Rooms · sponsors · execution

Pretty & Paid grew from years of planning women’s conferences, securing venues, building sponsor relationships, and learning how to create rooms that feel elevated, strategic, and deeply aligned with purpose.
